Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us, and our website. This privacy policy covers how we treats personal information that our website collects and receives, including information related to your past use of products and services. Personal information is information about you that is personally identifiable to you, such as your name, email address, or phone number, and that is not otherwise publicly available. Our website treats all personal information about you with great respect and takes various measures to protect your privacy and to secure all information about you. You may visit our website and may use various aspects of our Features and Services without revealing any personal information. Other aspects of our website, such as Features and Services, may allow you to provide us with personal information so that you can receive additional information. All personal information that you voluntarily submit or enter into the our system is kept confidential and certain third parties that help operate our service. Our website will not knowingly release any of your personal information to any other party unless you expressly permit it or if we are required to do so by law, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims.


We will comply with this privacy policy, as well as applicable U.S. privacy laws, when we gather and use information from users of our services. We encourage you to read this privacy policy carefully before you provide us with any personal information to be sure you understand what we do with any information we collect from you.


This privacy policy only applies to our website and does not apply to any companies or websites.


Information Collection and Use

Our website is an informational site which visitors can browse anonymously. We do not collect any personally identifiable information from the visitors to this site unless they register. Once you register with our website and sign in to use our services, you are not anonymous to us. When you registe, we collect a variety of information about you as explained more fully below.


When registering for services with our website, you may be asked to provide information about yourself, such as your name, email address, and phone number. This may occur when you register on our website or complete other forms, such as requests for property information. We may combine this information with other information we collect from your activity on our website or from other companies.


When you visit our website and use our services, we may place one or more “cookies” on your computer. A cookie is a small text file that is placed on your computer's hard drive. A cookie may contain information that allows us to track your path through our site and to determine whether you have visited us before. Unless you register with us, however, a cookie contains no personally identifiable information that would allow us to identify you. Cookies cannot be used to read data off of your hard drive, and cannot retrieve information from any other cookies created by other Web sites. The use of cookies helps us improve the quality of our services to you by identifying information which is most interesting to you, tracking trends, or storing information you may want to retrieve on a regular basis, such as your favorite properties. You may refuse to accept a cookie from us by following the procedures specific to your Web browser and adjusting settings on your Web browser. However, if you choose to disable cookies, many of the free features and services on our website will not operate properly.


When you use our website and services, your computer communicates a variety of information to computer servers to which we have access, including the existence of cookies, your Internet Protocol address, and information about your browser. We also create a record of what information you are requesting, such as the page or subject areas you are visiting.


Disclosure of your email address, and any other volunteered information, to a listing organization is entirely optional and is a service available for visitors who wish to receive information from a listing firm. We give all visitors the opportunity to "opt out" of email contact from a real estate agent.

Your privacy is important and we are committed to protecting information that identifies you personally where you have chosen to keep it private. Our website does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or non-affiliated companies except under the following circumstances:
